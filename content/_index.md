Hi, I'm Raven.

I spend most of my free time (which isn't a lot) writing open-source software. Almost
everything I make starts as a problem I ran into myself. If something feels repetitive,
awkward, or unclear, I usually end up building something to fix it.

Most of what I work on is in Rust or Python. A lot of it turns into small utilities,
libraries, or experiments that I keep refining over time. I like software that's
straightforward and does what it's supposed to without much noise.
